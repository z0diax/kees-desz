Our Love Story

A journey of love and devotion

How We Met

Our story didn't start with grand gestures or sweeping romance, it started with a laugh. Back in college, she was the clumsy girl who always trip over nothing and he was the grumpy guy who somehow always seemed annoyed. Due to a common friend their paths crossed in the school gym, hallways, and in small moments.

Life took them in different directions for a while, and they lost touch. However, as time would have it, they found each other again unexpectedly. This time, they saw each other clearly: not just the clumsy girl or the grumpy guy, but two people who made each other laugh, feel safe, and simply happy.

We discovered that love isn't about grand gestures it's about the small, everyday things: teasing each other, sharing silly jokes, offering comfort when days are tough, and celebrating the little victories together. Our journey has been filled with growth, patience, and countless memories that have shaped a bond stronger than either of them could have imagined.
